I'm a research assistant at the University of Kurdistan working on deep learning (from optimization and generalization aspects). I received my Master's in Artificial Intelligence from the Department of Computer, at University of Kurdistan in 2018. My advisors were Dr. Parham Moradi and Dr. Fardin Akhlaghian. My work mostly focused on the matrix factorization and low-rank approximation, predominantly for semi-supervised, recommendation, and multi-label applications. Before that, I obtained my Associate and Bachelor's degrees in Software Engineering.
