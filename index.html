I am a Doctoral Researcher at the University of Mons, supervised by Prof. Nicolas Gillis. My current research sits at the intersection of Matrix Theory and Optimization, specifically developing matrix factorization and low-rank approximation methods for Trustworthy Machine Learning. I aim to leverage these mathematical tools to enhance robustness, interpretability, and fairness in high-dimensional data and network analysis. Previously, I was a Graduate Research Assistant at the University of Kurdistan, where I led the Algebraic Machine Learning (AML) team and focused on representation learning. I hold an MSc in Artificial Intelligence from the same institution, where my work with Dr. Parham Moradi and Dr. Fardin Akhlaghian focused on matrix factorization for semi-supervised learning and recommender systems.
